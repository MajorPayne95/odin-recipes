Grilled Pork Chop

Make your mouth water with with perfectly grilled Pork chops! Perfect for the next bbq!

Ingredients

  • Thawed bone-in pork chops
  • Favorite chop seasoning
  • Grill

Instructions

  1. Use seasoning on both side of pork chop 5 to 20 minutes before grill time! (the longer the wait, the better!)
  2. Preheat grill to between 400F and 450F
  3. Place chops on grill and let cook for 5 minutes
  4. After 5 minutes, flip chops over and cook again for 5 minutes
  5. Flip chops over again, and cook an additional 3 minutes
  6. Flip one last time and cook for final 3 minutes to finish
  7. Remove chops from grill and let sit for 5 minutes before serving
