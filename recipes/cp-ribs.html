Crockpot Ribs

Ingredients

  • Crockpot
  • Rack of ribs
  • Pork dry rub seasoning
  • Favorite bbq sauce
  • TIME

Instructions

  1. Remove rib membrane (if applicable)
  2. Season ribs well with favorite pork seasoning
  3. Place ribs inside crockpot, meat-side facing out towards crockpot walls
  4. Cook on low for 6-8 hours depending on rack size and thickness
  5. Once meat starts to pull away from bone, pour bbq sauce over rack and brush to cover meat
  6. Remove from crockpot and apply more bbq sauce as wanted
