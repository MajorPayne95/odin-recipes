Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Ground Hamburger
  • Canned Spaghetti Sauce
  • Hamburger seasoning
  • Olive Oil
  • Large pot
  • Frying Pan
  • Water
  • Strainer

Instructions

  1. Fill large pot with water and bring to a boil
  2. Add noodles and some olive oil to the boiling water
  3. Boil noodles per cooking instructions described on box (usually 7-10 minutes)
  4. While noodles are boiling, heat up frying pad and cook ground beef until done (Don't forget to use your hamburger seasoning!!!)
  5. Once noodles are finished, drain out water with strainer
  6. Combine noodles and meat using the large pot
  7. Add spaghetti sauce and mix together well
  8. Serve and enjoy
